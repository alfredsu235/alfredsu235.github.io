Final Project

In this program we made, we wanted to make a simple RPG game where the game is straightforward and simple. Where anyone can understand the game and have fun with it at the same time. You will have two attributes to apply your point to. Either Strength or Weapon Multiplier. How the attributes works is they multiply each other combing the total ammount of attacking damage you do. By clicking on the monster you can attack, and the goal is to get as much kills as you can before you die.
